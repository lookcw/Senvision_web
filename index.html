Trading stocks on Twitter data

A working approach on trading on news data.

We have created an algorithm that trades based on Twitter data. We spent a year and iterated through five algorithms to reach this point. We have beat the market with this algorithm, not only using backtests, but using our own money. Sentiment Analysis extends beyond technical analysis to poll the internet's opinion of companies.

We made money with our algorithm.

In one month we made almost 10% returns (11/19/17 - 12/19/17) This is our own hard earned money. We trust it because we have seen its results.

And we beat the benchmark.

During the same time, the S&P 500 made only 4.2%.

How we do it

We collect data from twitter and predict on 8 companies, listed below. Each day the algorithm predicts which stocks will go up, and which will go down and trade automatically based on that. We can choose any company we like, but we just have these 8 for simplicity and because the twitter API is not free.
